About ChopeTissue

We didn’t invent the tissue. We reinvented the chope.

Our Story

It started at a crowded Maxwell Food Centre. One of our founders, desperate to secure a table, placed a sad, crumpled tissue packet on the seat – and thought, “This deserves more style.” That moment sparked an obsession: why shouldn’t the everyday tissue packet be a canvas for Singaporean art? ChopeTissue was born to turn a humble tradition into a premium experience.

Today we work with local artists to design limited‑edition tissue packets that celebrate our heritage – from the Merlion to HDB void decks. Every subscription box is a tiny art gallery that also helps you chope your favourite spot.

Frequently Asked Questions

What exactly is a “seat-reservation tissue subscription”?
It’s a monthly delivery of beautifully designed tissue packets used to chope (reserve) your seat at hawker centres and food courts – a beloved Singaporean tradition, now upgraded.
How do I choose my tissue designs?
Every month we release a new curated collection. Subscribers can personalise by selecting from our current range before the 15th.
Are the tissues eco‑friendly?
Absolutely. FSC‑certified, 100% biodegradable, printed with soy‑based inks. Even the packaging is compostable.
